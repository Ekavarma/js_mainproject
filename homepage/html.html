            ABOUT US  
            Welcome to Sweet Scoops Ice Cream Parlor, your ultimate destination for indulgent and refreshing ice cream experiences!  
            Located in the heart of Bengaluru, India, at   123 Ice Cream Lane, MG Road  , our parlor is a haven for ice cream lovers of all ages.
            
            At Sweet Scoops, we pride ourselves on offering a wide variety of ice creams to cater to every taste and preference. Our menu features:  
            -   Classic Flavors   like Vanilla, Chocolate, and Strawberry.  
            -   Fruity Delights   with mango, blueberry, and mixed berry.  
            -   Rich Chocolate Creations  , including Belgian Chocolate, Choco Lava, and Dark Truffle.  
            -   Nutty Wonders such as Pistachio, Caramel Almond, and Cashew Delight.  
            -   Seasonal Specials  , inspired by the freshest ingredients available, including fresh Alphonso Mango and Summer Watermelon Sorbet.  
            
            Every scoop is crafted with premium ingredients, ensuring a burst of flavor in every bite. Our ice creams are made fresh daily, 
            combining traditional techniques with innovative ideas to create unique and satisfying experiences.
